We're mixing things up!

The landscape in eCommerce is rapidly evolving to one in rich user-experiences that engage customers and inspire. We're gathering industry experts, cutting-edge tech companies, and world class agencies together to talk about what's next... and have some fun in the process.

Join us for an informative evening of conversation and drinks, the night before the biggest event of the year in the Drupal Community, DrupalCon Los Angeles.

Why Attend?

Commerce Revolution is a great opportunity to learn from other companies who have made the move towards content driven eCommerce. Its a chance to ask questions and find out how Drupal Commerce can fit into your company's plan for growth. Not to mention a great chance to have some fun and share a few drinks with the Creators of & Experts in Drupal Commerce.

You should consider attending if:

  • You have already invested in Drupal, and want to find out how you can get a greater return by serving your commerce needs
  • Your considering replatforming your eCommerce site and want to free yourself from an inflexible solution & license fees
  • You have atypical eCommerce products, like subscriptions, memberships or digital goods to sell.
About the Revolution

The Commerce Revolution was born out of an idea to bring together thought leaders in open-source eCommerce and provide a forum for business executives and IT professionals to discuss the shift in eCommerce today. But also to have a bit of fun at the same time! The first of an ongoing series of events, the Commerce Revolution is a great opportunity to network with like minded companies and discover new ways to grow your business. Come prepared to learn ... AND have fun!

Location

In the heart of Downtown Los Angeles, steps from DrupalCon LA


The Hotel Figurero serves as the backdrop for Commerce Revolution (but it may very well steal the show!) Host to some of the most unique architecture in L.A. ,the location is unexpected oasis amidst an urban metropolis. The venue just a stone’s throw from L.A. Live, Staples Center, and the Los Angeles Convention Center. This beloved downtown LA hotel is a true classic, with dramatic décor of rich colors and intricate patterns that's been featured in Hollywood films and played host to countless events.

Come to network and grow your business... and be wowed by the amazing decor and food!
